# Kheper

**"Talk it into existence. Sync it to your day."**

---

## 📋 Overview
**Kheper** is a high-efficiency, cross-platform productivity tool designed to bridge the gap between spoken thoughts and actionable tasks. By leveraging **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**, SET listens to your voice messages and automatically transforms them into a structured daily schedule, synchronized across all your devices via a secure email-based account.

---

## 🛠 How It Works
1. **Secure Identity:** Log in with your email and password. Your account acts as a centralized hub for all your tasks.
2. **Listen & Parse:** Use the voice interface to dictate your plans. SET’s **NLP engine** filters the noise to identify specific actions.
3. **Automatic Distribution:** The "Synchronized" core ensures that a task captured on your phone is instantly visible on your web dashboard and desktop app.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎙️ NLP-Driven Task Extraction
Forget simple transcriptions. SET analyzes the context of your speech to identify:
* **Actions:** What needs to be done.
* **Deadlines:** Recognizes phrases like *"in two hours,"* *"tomorrow morning,"* or *"next Monday."*
* **Priorities:** Detects urgency based on your tone and word choice.

### 🔄 Multi-Platform Synchronization
As the name **Synchronized Email ToDo** suggests, your data is never siloed.
* **Mobile:** Capture tasks on the go.
* **Web:** Manage your day from any browser.
* **Desktop:** Keep your list pinned to your workspace.

### 📧 Email-Centric Architecture
Your email isn't just a login; it's the anchor of your productivity. Receive daily summaries and critical alerts directly to your inbox to ensure nothing slips through the cracks.

---

## 🎯 Why Choose Kheper?
Most task managers fail because manual entry is a chore. **kheper** removes the friction:
* **Zero Typing:** Create a full day's plan while driving or walking.
* **Context-Aware:** It understands *how* you speak, not just *what* you say.
* **Unified:** One account, all platforms, total synchronization.

---

> **Mission Statement:** To simplify human productivity by converting natural speech into a synchronized, actionable reality.
